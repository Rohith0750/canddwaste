Innovative Solutions for Managing Construction and Demolition Waste Using Raspberry Pi

This research explores innovative solutions for managing construction and demolition (C&D) waste, specifically using Raspberry Pi technology. Raspberry Pi is being utilized for automation, monitoring, and managing the waste generated from construction activities. The system leverages sensors and data analytics to ensure efficient waste management practices, thus minimizing environmental impacts and promoting recycling.

The study provides insights into how Raspberry Pi can be employed to monitor waste levels, track the types of materials being discarded, and optimize sorting processes, ensuring that recyclable materials are handled effectively. Additionally, it highlights the potential for integrating smart waste management systems into large-scale construction projects to streamline operations and reduce costs.

Key Areas of Research:

  • Automation in waste tracking and sorting
  • Integration of sensor technology for real-time waste data collection
  • Optimizing recycling processes using data analytics
  • Environmental and economic benefits of using Raspberry Pi in construction waste management
